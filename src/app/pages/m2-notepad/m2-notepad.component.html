Nutrient Digestibility & Energy​

Dig NDF – digestible NDF 
  • Key source of energy for the cow and drives rumen fermentation.
  • Accurately predicts nutrient supply based on extent and rate of NDF digestion.
RumDig Starch –rumen digestible starch​
  • Key source of energy for the cow and drives rumen fermentation.
  • Calculates both chemical and physical characteristics of starch sources to more accurately predict what happens in the cow, improving animal health and performance.​
Rumen VFA’s – prediction of Volatile Fatty Acid production in rumen from fermentation.
  • A key part of our total energy calculation that provides insight for optimizing rumen fermentation.​
NEL Dairy – Net Energy of Lactation​
  • Proprietary energy model provides an accurate understanding of energy content in the diet which is a key driver of animal performance.​

Amino Acids

DDAA’s (Histidine, Lysine, Methionine, Phenylalanine + other essentials) – dairy digestible amino acids​
  • Accurately calculates the supply of amino acids available to dairy cows which drive milk production, milk composition, and producer profitability. ​
  • Optimizes the cost of supplying key amino acids through a “rumen first” approach to maximize rumen output of microbial protein.​

Milk Composition​

Milk Fat Index – Cargill model​
  • Predicts changes in milk fat composition in different diets to help customers optimize profitability and income over feed cost while providing insight on milk composition changes.
BARAL – Bioactive Rumen Available Fatty Acid Load​​
  • Provides a way to troubleshoot milk fat depression issues and the impact of diet changes by looking at the unsaturated fats in the diet that can impact the milk fat %.​

Rumen Environment​

rH Index – rumen health index​
  • Predicts rumen pH and provides insight into the risk level of rumen acidosis in the cow. ​
  • Optimizes rumen function and maximizes rumen performance and health.​
Methane – part of the rumen fermentation model​
  • Predicts amount of methane produced by the rumen and is a key component of sustainability. ​
  • Looks at wasted energy during the rumen fermentation process.​​
Transition Cow Nutrition – balanced rumen during transition phase ​
  • Focuses on correct balance of Dig NDF, Starch, and Sugar to transition rumen environment.​​
  • Minimizes Dry Matter Intake (DMI) decrease through calving leading to earlier and higher peak milk. ​
