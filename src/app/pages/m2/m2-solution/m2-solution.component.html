Solution

Isabela evaluated the cows and the diet. The evaluation revealed there was too much starch in the diet, so she focused much of her solution on adjusting the diet.

She used dairy MAX to adjust the diet focusing mostly on rumen function (adjusted total starch, Rumen Health Index, RDP and forage inclusion rate). She also adjusted the required energy (NEI Dairy) and protein (DDAA) needed.

Results

    After adjusting the diet in dairy MAX the results were positive:
  • Manure score went from 2 to 3 after 16 days.
  • Milk yield increased from 33.5 liters to 37.9 liters per cow/day.
  • Feeding costs were reduced in each group
