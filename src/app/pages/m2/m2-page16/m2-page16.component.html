Lab – Calculated nutrients


Once the nutrients have been analyzed and defined, they are calculated in an engine called AutoCalc to further describe key values that are not measured in the lab.

Here is an example:

Energy for lactating dairy cows is expressed as a calculated nutrient known as Net Energy of Lactation (NEL Dairy). This value cannot be measured directly in the lab, it needs to be obtained by:

  • Analyzing the ingredient content of protein, NDF, fat, starch, sugar, etc.
  • Predicting the digestibility of these nutrients in the rumen or intestine.

This calculation takes animal and diet characteristics into account and predicts how the nutrients will be metabolized by the animal – or in this case how it is converted into NEL.

Calculations in CNS are updated every 6 – 12 months and some of these calculated nutrients are proprietary to Cargill.
