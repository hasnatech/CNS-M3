Lab – Analyzed nutrients

Two groups of nutrients are analyzed in the lab

1. Basic nutrients which are commonly used to describe ingredients sent to the lab including:  

  • Dry matter
  • NDF (Neutral Detergent Fiber)
  • Ash
  • Protein
  • Fat

2. More specific nutrients are used if the ingredient is a major source of fiber, starch, or protein* and the nutrient content and digestibility needs to be further defined:

  • NDF extent (NDF extent of digestion)
  • cStarch (Cargill Starch)
  • Soluble RDP (Rumen Degradable Protein)
  • IVAAD (In Vitro Amino Acid Digestibility)
  • NDF Kd (NDF rate of digestion)
  • Rum Sugar (Ruminant Sugar)
  • PreRUP (Rumen Undegraded Protein)

*Other specific nutrients vary by region. For detailed information of key nutrients available in your area, contact your local SMT technical lead.
